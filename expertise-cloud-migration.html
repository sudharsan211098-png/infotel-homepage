Our Process

Our Streamlined Cloud Migration Process

From legacy infrastructure to cloud-native — a structured, phased approach that minimises risk and maximises continuity at every step.

1

Strategy Defining

We assess your business goals, migration triggers, and workload priorities to define and establish a clear cloud strategy aligned to your business objectives.

2

Assess Current Infrastructure

We audit your existing IT estate — applications, data, dependencies, and performance baselines — to build a complete picture of what moves, what transforms, and what stays.

3

Plan Migration

We build a holistic migration plan covering priorities, timelines, milestones, resources, and budget. Careful coordination here ensures minimal downtime and a seamless transition.

4

Prepare Your Organisation

Our cloud experts upskill your development teams on cloud-enabled technologies, migration tooling, and new operational processes to ensure readiness before migration begins.

5

Implement Migration

Migration is executed in three phases: Infrastructure Migration, Application Migration, and Data Migration — each handled in waves using your chosen migration strategy.

6

Secure and Manage

We help safeguard workloads across all cloud environments using your cloud provider's security toolset — firewalls, intrusion detection, monitoring, and ongoing governance.

Migration Strategy

Choose the Right Migration Strategy

Unlock the full potential of cloud technology with an approach tailored to your application landscape, risk tolerance, and business goals.

01

Rehost

Transfer your application to the cloud environment with minimal architectural changes. The fastest path to cloud with immediate infrastructure cost savings.

02

Replatform

Optimise your application for the cloud to improve productivity and scalability — without changing core architecture. A targeted performance upgrade.

03

Refactor

Modify applications to fully support the cloud environment. Involves breaking monolithic applications into microservices for greater scalability and resilience.

04

Revise

Build upon replatforming and refactoring by making more profound changes to system architecture and codebase — enabling deeper cloud-native capability.

05

Rebuild

Discard or replace existing code entirely, allowing businesses to leverage innovative cloud-native features and build for future scale from the ground up.

FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Cloud Migration Services refer to the process of transferring data, applications, and other business elements from on-premises infrastructure to cloud environments — whether public, private, or hybrid — with minimal disruption to operations.

Cloud migration enhances efficiency by automating processes and enabling real-time remote access, allowing teams to collaborate from anywhere. It also eliminates hardware refresh cycles, speeds up deployment, and scales resources to match demand instantly.

Cloud migration is a highly secure process when managed correctly. We implement robust security measures including data encryption, multi-factor authentication, identity and access management, and regular security audits throughout the migration lifecycle.

Businesses should assess their specific workload needs, the type of cloud environment (private, public, or hybrid), total cost of ownership, regulatory and compliance requirements, and the internal technical expertise available before committing to a migration plan.

AWS is ideal if you need the broadest range of services and global infrastructure. Microsoft Azure is the strongest choice for organisations running Microsoft or Windows-based systems. Google Cloud Platform is best suited for AI, machine learning, and data-intensive workloads. We help you evaluate and choose the right fit.
